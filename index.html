About this tool

This open-source data cleaning, analysis, and visualization tool is designed to streamline your workflow and make data-related tasks significantly easier. It empowers you to gain deep insights into your data by providing comprehensive statistical analysis, including key metrics like mean, mode, median, and identification of NaN (Not a Number) values and outliers.

Beyond analysis, the tool offers robust data cleaning capabilities. You can effortlessly handle missing data by filling or removing NaN values, apply custom removal conditions based on your specific needs, and manage outliers to ensure data accuracy. The tool also allows you to compare your data before and after cleaning, giving you a clear view of the improvements made.

A key advantage of this tool is its local operation. All processing occurs directly within your browser, ensuring the privacy and security of your data. Because it works locally, you can even use it offline once the page has loaded, allowing you to analyze and clean countless datasets without an internet connection.

Currently, the tool supports tabular data formats, including CSV, XLSX, XLS, and JSON. If your data is in a different format, you can easily convert it to a supported format and then upload it to the tool for analysis. There are no limitations on file size or the number of attempts, giving you the freedom to work with your data as needed.
